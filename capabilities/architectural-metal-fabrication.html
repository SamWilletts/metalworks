Facilities

Workshop

The Metalworks team operates from a central site, located in the heart of Dunedin’s industrial area. Our workshop, sized at approximately 1,000 square metres and utilising two overhead gantry cranes, ensures we have the capability to pre-fabricate large projects before site installations. You can find us here.

Machinery

Our workshop houses specialised fabrication and assembly machinery – from an ercolina mandrel tube-bender and sectional rollers, to a 160T press brake. Access to this machinery means we have the capability to fabricate a number of projects on site. We also have access to laser and plasma cutting machinery. To find out if we have the machinery to get your job done, contact us today. Or take a look at our services and capabilities.
