Louvres

Metalworks Otago works in collaboration with Insol and Aurae (architectural facade companies), to install custom-made louvre systems for commercial and residential premises.

Commercial Louvres

Metalworks Otago are the exclusive certified installer for Insol commercial louvre systems across the region south of Timaru. Our team is experienced in delivering high-quality installations for a variety of commercial applications, ensuring top performance and durability. Find more information on Insol louvres here.

Residential Louvres

As a certified installer for Aurae residential louvre systems, we provide expert installation services for residential properties. We focus on enhancing the functionality and aesthetic appeal with Aurae's innovative louvre solutions. Find more information on Aurae louvres here.
