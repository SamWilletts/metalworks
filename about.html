About us

Focused on delivering exceptional engineering solutions, we take pride in providing expert advice to ensure positive outcomes for all projects.

Whether you are looking for structural, architectural or general metalwork, we will work with you to deliver high-quality results.

Engineering Experience & Expertise

We have a range of highly skilled engineers who have many years of experience, specialising in a broad spectrum of engineering solutions.

Quality of Workmanship

We take pride in ensuring all our projects reflect our standard for high quality workmanship – that means we pay attention to the small details and the finest of finishing.

Health & Safety Standards

At Metalworks Otago, we put safety first. We have a Gold Sitewise accreditation, which showcases our dedication and commitment to the highest safety standards and practices.

Communication

We know that keeping our clients informed and updated throughout the project lifecycle is critical. We will work with you to understand your requirements and expectations, and keep you informed along the way.

Efficiency

For us, we pride ourselves on our ability to work to your deadlines and deliver the project as expected – cost effectively, of a high quality, and on time.

Workshop

The Metalworks team operates from a central site, located in the heart of Dunedin’s industrial area. Our workshop, sized at approximately 1,000 square metres and utilising two overhead gantry cranes, ensures we have the capability to pre-fabricate large projects before site installations. You can find us here.

Machinery

Our workshop houses specialised fabrication and assembly machinery – from an ercolina mandrel tube-bender and sectional rollers, to a 160T press brake. Access to this machinery means we have the capability to fabricate a number of projects on site. We also have access to laser and plasma cutting machinery. To find out if we have the machinery to get your job done, contact us today. Or read about our services and capabilities.

Our team is comprised of experienced engineers with a high degree of attention to detail and a wide range of capabilities.
